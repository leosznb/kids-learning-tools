    3.2 Theme 2: Tropical Rainforests 熱帶雨林

    Distribution & Location 分布與位置

    ElementDetails
    LatitudesMainly between 10°N and 10°S; extend to 23.5°N and 23.5°S where climatic conditions are favourable 主要位於南北緯10°之間；氣候條件允許時可延伸至回歸線
    Three main regions1. Amazon Basin (South America) — largest, ~50–73% of world total 亞馬遜盆地（南美洲）— 最大
    2. Congo Basin (Africa) 剛果盆地（非洲）
    3. Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Malaysia, etc.) 東南亞
    Why near Equator?Low latitudes → high angle of sun → large amount of solar radiation → hot & wet all year 低緯度太陽高度角大→太陽輻射強→終年高溫多雨

    Climatic Characteristics 氣候特徵

    ElementCharacteristicExplanation
    Annual rainfall> 2,000 mm; rains almost every day, usually in the afternoon 年降水量>2000毫米；幾乎每日有雨，多在下午Convection rain: intense heating → air rises → cools → condenses → rainfall 對流雨
    Annual mean temperatureAbove 26°C 年均溫高於26°CHigh solar radiation throughout the year 全年太陽輻射強
    Annual range of temperatureSmall, only 2–4°C 年溫差小No winter season; consistently high insolation 無冬季；日照持續強烈
    Relative humidityHigh (above 90%) 相對濕度高Abundant rainfall + high temperature → much evapotranspiration 豐沛降雨+高溫→大量蒸散
    Past Paper Trap 歷年陷阱題： HK has no tropical rainforest because it is located further north (beyond 10°N), affected by the monsoon system → distinct wet & dry seasons, lower temperature & rainfall.
    香港無熱帶雨林，因緯度較高（北回歸線以北），受季風影響，有明顯乾濕季，氣溫與雨量不足。
    Marking Scheme Tip (2324 Q2d / 2425 Q2c): For "describe & explain temperature", any 4 of: (1) high temp (>26°C), (2) low latitudes, (3) large solar radiation, (4) high angle of sun, (5) concentrated insolation, (6) no winter = full marks.

    Five-Layered Vegetation Structure 五層植被結構

    LayerNameHeightCharacteristics
    1Emergent layer 露生層> 50 mTallest trees rise above canopy; straight thick trunks; buttress roots; compete for sunlight 最高大樹突出冠層之上；筆直粗壯樹幹；板根支撐
    2Canopy layer 冠層20–35 mDense, continuous cover of broad leaves; blocks most sunlight 茂密連續的闊葉覆蓋；遮擋大部分陽光
    3Understorey 幼樹層—Young trees waiting to reach canopy 等待長至高層的幼樹
    4Shrub layer 灌木層—Shrubs, small trees 灌木、小樹
    5Undergrowth & ground layer 底層—Dark & wet; sparse vegetation; ferns, herbs, mosses, saprophytes, rafflesia 陰暗潮濕；植被稀疏；蕨類、草本植物、苔蘚、腐生植物、萊佛士花

    Plant Adaptations 植物適應性

    AdaptationFunction
    Buttress roots 板根Support tall trees in shallow soil 支撐高大樹木
    Broad leaves 闊葉Maximize sunlight interception for photosynthesis 最大化截獲陽光進行光合作用
    Drip-tips 滴水葉尖Drain excess water quickly 快速排走多餘雨水
    Waxy leaf surfaces 蠟質葉面Reduce evapotranspiration 減少蒸散
    Shallow roots 淺根Absorb nutrients before leaching 在養分流失前吸收
    Smooth, thin, straight trunks 光滑筆直樹幹Grow tall rapidly to reach sunlight 快速長高以獲取陽光
    Evergreen 常綠No marked dry/cool season → leaves shed & regrow continuously 無明顯乾旱或寒冷季節，全年持續落葉與生長

    Special Plant Types 特殊植物類型

    TypeDefinitionExample
    Epiphytes 附生植物Grow on other plants for physical support only; NOT parasites 僅依附其他植物生長，非寄生Orchids, ferns 蘭花、蕨類
    Parasites 寄生植物Obtain nutrients from host plant 從寄主植物獲取養分Rafflesia 萊佛士花
    Climbers 攀緣植物Climb up trees to reach sunlight 攀附樹木向上生長以獲取陽光Lianas 藤蔓
    Saprophytes 腐生植物Feed on dead organic matter 以死亡有機物為食Mushrooms, fungi 蘑菇、真菌

    Roles of Rainforests in Global Climate 雨林調節全球氣候的角色

    Carbon sink 碳匯: Absorb CO₂ during photosynthesis · Oxygen source 氧氣來源: Release O₂ · Evapotranspiration 蒸散: Release water vapour → form clouds → rainfall · Regulate temperature 調節氣溫: Dense vegetation absorbs heat

    Resources from Rainforests 雨林資源

    CategoryExamples
    Food 食物Cacao/cocoa, banana, maize, oil palms, pineapple 可可、香蕉、玉米、油棕、菠蘿
    Medicine 藥物Medicinal plants collected by native people 原住民採集的藥用植物
    Timber & wood 木材Hardwood for furniture & construction 硬木用於家具與建築
    Natural rubber 天然橡膠Rubber bands, tyres 橡皮筋、輪胎
    Biofuel 生物燃料Oil palms, maize 油棕、玉米
    Genetic resources 基因資源Biodiversity for research & breeding 生物多樣性供研究與育種

    3.3 Theme 3: Deforestation 森林砍伐

    Causes of Large-Scale Deforestation 大規模砍伐原因

    LevelCauses
    Global / Economic 全球/經濟層面Rising standard of living → higher demand for meat, consumer goods, biofuel 生活水平提高→需求增加
    Most rainforest countries are poor → governments exploit rainforests to repay foreign debts 雨林國家貧窮→開發雨林償還外債
    National 國家層面Rapid population growth → demand for natural resources 人口快速增長→自然資源需求
    Need for economic development → roads, dams, towns 經濟發展需要→修路、建壩、建城
    Local / Activity 地方/活動層面Shifting cultivation (slash-and-burn) 游耕（刀耕火種）· Cattle ranching / livestock rearing 畜牧養殖 · Plantations (oil palms, soybean, banana) 種植園 · Commercial logging 商業伐木 · Opencast mining 露天採礦 · Firewood collection 薪柴採集
    Marking Scheme Tip (2324 Q3b): Underlying causes = 3 marks. Must mention: population growth + demand for resources, rising living standard + demand for meat/goods, poor countries + foreign debts.

    Environmental Impacts 環境影響

    ImpactExplanation
    Loss of biodiversity / habitats 生物多樣性/棲息地喪失Destruction of five-layered structure → wildlife extinction 破壞五層結構→野生動物滅絕
    Soil erosion & flooding 土壤侵蝕與洪水Tree roots no longer bind soil; topsoil washed away by rain 樹根不再固土；表土被雨水沖走
    Disruption of water cycle 水循環破壞Less evapotranspiration → less cloud & rainfall 蒸散減少→雲層與降雨減少
    Changes in local climate 局地氣候改變Drier microclimate; larger diurnal temperature range; stronger winds 微氣候變乾；日溫差增大；風力增強
    Global warming 全球暖化Loss of carbon sink → more CO₂ in atmosphere 碳匯喪失→大氣二氧化碳增加
    Loss of genetic resources / medicines 基因資源/藥物喪失Potential cures lost before discovery 潛在藥物未發現即消失

    Socio-Economic Impacts on Native People 對原住民的社會經濟影響

    Loss of homes & social ties 失去家園與社會聯繫 · Decrease in food & water supply 食物與水源減少 · Spread of infectious diseases 傳染病蔓延 · Loss of unique culture / traditions 獨特文化/傳統喪失 · Environmental pollution (water, air) 環境污染 · More extreme weather events 更極端天氣事件

    Conservation Measures 保護措施

    StakeholderMeasures
    Governments 政府Land use planning / establish national parks & nature reserves 設立國家公園與自然保護區 · Legislation to ban illegal logging 立法禁止非法砍伐 · Timber certification (e.g. FSC) 木材認證 · Afforestation / reforestation 植樹造林 · Carbon trading & carbon taxation 碳交易與碳稅 · Population control 人口控制 · Improving governance & fighting corruption 改善治理與打擊貪腐
    Private corporations 私營企業Better logging methods: selective logging 選擇性砍伐 · Corporate social responsibility (CSR) 企業社會責任 · Develop agroforestry 農林複合經營 · Support products with eco-labels 支持環保標籤產品
    Individuals 個人Simple lifestyle: reduce, reuse, recycle 簡樸生活：減少使用、重複利用、循環再造 · Smart consumption: choose certified / eco-labelled products 明智消費 · Eat less meat 減少肉食（降低畜牧與飼料種植需求）

    Constraints of eco-labels: Higher costs, little choice, lack of promotion / monitoring. 環保標籤限制：成本較高、選擇較少、推廣與監管不足。

    Marking Scheme Tip (2324 Q3h): Government measures = 2 marks each for any 2 valid suggestions. Must be government-level actions (not individual or corporate).

    Deforestation Cause-Impact-Measure Chain 砍伐因果鏈

    Population growth + Rising living standard
    ↓
    Higher demand for meat, timber, farmland, biofuel
    ↓
    Shifting cultivation / cattle ranching / logging / mining
    ↓
    Loss of forest cover
    ↓
    Env impacts: biodiversity loss, soil erosion, climate change
    Socio-economic impacts: native people displaced, culture lost
    ↓
    Solutions: Govt (parks, laws, carbon tax) + Corp (selective logging, CSR) + Individual (reduce, reuse, recycle, less meat)

    4. Question Type Breakdown & Answering Strategies 題型拆解與答題策略

    Section A: MCQ (20 marks)

    Strategy: Most questions are "Which of the following is/are correct?" with 3 statements → test all combinations carefully. 多數為「以下哪些正確？」含3個陳述→小心驗證所有組合。For "NOT" questions, eliminate obviously correct options first. 遇到「NOT」題型，先排除明顯正確的選項。

    Common traps 常見陷阱： Confuse carbon sink vs carbon source 碳匯與碳源 · epiphytes vs parasites 附生與寄生 · core vs integrated protection area 核心與綜合保護區 · igneous intrusive vs extrusive 侵入岩與噴出岩

    Section B: Structured Questions (66–68 marks)

    Sub-typeMarksStrategyMarking Scheme Pattern
    Identify / Name 辨識/命名1–2Use precise geographical terms 使用精確地理術語Exact term required; spelling errors may be penalised
    Describe 描述2–4State the trend/pattern first, then support with data 先說趨勢/模式，再以數據支持1 mark for overall trend + 1 mark for data support
    Explain 解釋2–4Use "Because… / This is due to…" structure. Link cause → process → effect 使用因果結構1 mark per valid linked point (cause + process + effect)
    Compare / Contrast 比較4–10Use a table format. Compare point-by-point (rock type, crystal, structure, resistance, landform) 善用答題紙表格，逐點比對1 mark per valid comparison point; sketches with labels = extra marks
    Calculate 計算1–3Always show steps: formula → substitution → answer with unit 必須展示步驟Step marks awarded even if final answer is wrong
    Discuss / Suggest 討論/建議2–4Give two-sided answer where appropriate 適當時給雙面論點1 mark per valid reason; must be well-explained
    Measure 測量2–4State tool (ruler), measurement (cm), scale, calculation, final answer with unit 說明工具、讀數、比例尺、計算±0.3 cm tolerance for map measurements

    Section C: Short Essay (14 marks) — PEEL Formula

    ComponentMarksRequirement
    Introduction1–2Topic sentence + elaboration
    Main Body Point 14Explain formation / describe characteristics with details
    Main Body Point 24Explain formation / discuss issue with examples
