第1篇：A Young Chef's Dream

U3 Dream jobs🎯 定位法难度：★☆☆

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

先认识这5个词，扫清阅读障碍。点击看中文。

chef 厨师
delicious 美味的
ingredient 食材
recipe 食谱
practise 练习

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① How old was Jason when he started cooking?
② What does Jason want to be in the future?

Jason is a Primary 5 student. He started cooking when he was seven years old. His grandmother taught him how to make fried rice. At first, the rice was too salty, but Jason did not give up.

Now Jason enjoys cooking very much. He loves making new dishes for his family. Every Saturday, he watches cooking videos on YouTube. He writes down the ingredients in a small notebook. His favourite dish to cook is spaghetti with meatballs.

Jason's friends think he is really good at cooking. Last month, he made a chocolate cake for his mother's birthday. Everyone said it was delicious.

Jason wants to be a chef in the future. He knows he needs to practise every day. He also would like to learn how to make different kinds of food from around the world. "Cooking makes me feel happy," Jason says. "I have never been bored in the kitchen."

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防（10分钟）

🎯 定位法

技巧核心：题目里的关键词 = 你在文章里要找的词。找到了就划线，答案就在附近！

1a定位法
How old was Jason when he started cooking?
🔑 题目关键词：how old + started cooking
✅ He started cooking when he was seven years old.
💡 第一段就有「started cooking when he was seven years old」——几乎一模一样！
1b定位法
What does Jason want to be in the future?
🔑 关键词：want to be + future
✅ He wants to be a chef in the future.
💡 最后一段第一句。wants to be → 注意三单！
1c定位法
Where does Jason write down the ingredients?
🔑 关键词：write down + ingredients
✅ He writes down the ingredients in a small notebook.
💡 第二段中后部。in a small notebook = 在一个小笔记本里。
1d定位法
What did everyone say about Jason's chocolate cake?
🔑 关键词：everyone + said + chocolate cake
✅ Everyone said it was delicious.
💡 第三段最后一句就是答案。

✍️ 环节四：完整句子四步法（8分钟）

1找在文章中找到答案句
2抄把原句抄下来
3改人称Jason→He, my→his
4写整句大写开头+句号结尾
1e人称转换
What does Jason enjoy doing every Saturday?
⚠️ 文章第二段：enjoys cooking, loves making, watches videos…
✅ He enjoys watching cooking videos on YouTube every Saturday.
💡 enjoy 后要加 -ing！这也是 U3 的语法点。
1f人称转换
Why does Jason need to practise every day?
⚠️ 把两处信息拼成一句，用 because 连接
✅ He needs to practise every day because he wants to be a chef in the future.
💡 最后一段两句话拼起来。这也是考试常见的2分题。

💎 环节五：一篇一得（5分钟）

从文章里活学 U3 语法：-ing form & to-infinitive

V-ing
enjoys cooking / loves making / good at cooking
to-infinitive
wants to be / needs to practise / would like to learn
✏️ 你来造两个句子（用你自己的生活）：
① I enjoy __________ .
② I want to __________ .

👨‍🏫 Ethan：今天怎么带多多？

  • 环节一：让他点开5个词看意思。问问「chef 和 cook 有什么不一样？」
  • 环节二：让他先读两道题，再读文章。读完问他「第一题答案在第几行？」——他应该能指出第一段。
  • 环节三：每题让他先说出「关键词是什么？」然后在文章里找到含这个词的句子。他答对了要大力夸！
  • 环节四：特别强调四步法。检查他的答案有没有大写开头、句号结尾。
  • 环节五：让他用自己生活造句。比如「I enjoy playing Roblox.」「I want to eat ice cream.」

💡 今天的核心目标：让多多体验到「题目问什么→文章里找什么→找到了就能答对」的成就感。

📝 今天学到的

✅ 定位法：题目关键词 → 文章找同样词 → 划线 → 抄下来
✅ 四步法：找 → 抄 → 改人称 → 写整句
✅ 语法活学：enjoy + V-ing / want + to + V

第2篇：The Nurse Who Never Gave Up（永不言弃的护士）

U4 Admiring people🎯 排除法难度：★★☆

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

admire 敬佩
patient 病人
award 奖项
kind 善良的
hard-working 勤奋的

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① How long has Aunt May been a nurse?
② Why does the writer admire Aunt May?

My Aunt May is a nurse. She has worked at Queen Elizabeth Hospital for 20 years. She has helped thousands of patients. Many of them still remember her kind smile.

Aunt May has been a nurse since she was 22 years old. When she was young, many people told her that nursing was a difficult job. But Aunt May never gave up. She wanted to help sick people get better.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, Aunt May worked very long hours. She sometimes did not go home for several days. She took care of many very sick patients. Although she was tired, she never complained. She said, "This is my job, and I am proud of it."

Last year, Aunt May won the Best Nurse Award at her hospital. Everyone clapped for her. I have admired her since I was a child. I hope I can be as brave and kind as her one day.

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防 — 今天学排除法！（10分钟）

🎯 排除法

技巧核心：选择题不会做？先划掉肯定错的，剩下的就是对的！
三个排除方向：① 文章里根本没提的 → ❌  ② 和文章相反的 → ❌  ③ 只有一半对的 → ❌

2a排除法（MC）
How long has Aunt May worked at Queen Elizabeth Hospital?
A. For 12 years
B. For 20 years
C. Since she was 20
D. Since last year
✅ B. For 20 years
💡 第一段：has worked at QEH for 20 years。
❌ A：12 years — 文章没有 12。 ❌ C：since she was 20 — 文章是 since she was 22。 ❌ D：since last year — last year 是她拿奖，不是开始工作。
2b排除法（MC）
What did Aunt May do during the COVID-19 pandemic?
A. She stayed at home for several days
B. She took care of many sick patients
C. She went to another country
D. She won an award
✅ B. She took care of many sick patients
💡 第三段原文。 ❌ A — 文章说「did NOT go home」，刚好相反！ ❌ C — 文章没提。 ❌ D — 获奖是 last year，不是 pandemic 期间。
2c排除法 + 定位
When did Aunt May become a nurse?
A. 20 years ago
B. When she was 18
C. Since last year
D. When she was 22
✅ D. When she was 22
💡 第二段：since she was 22 years old。
❌ A — 是「for 20 years」但不是「20 years ago」——注意 for 和 ago 的区别！ ❌ C — last year 是拿奖时间。
2d排除法
What happened to Aunt May last year?
A. She started working at the hospital
B. She got married
C. She won the Best Nurse Award
D. She became a patient
✅ C. She won the Best Nurse Award
💡 最后一段：Last year…won the Best Nurse Award。 ❌ A — 20年前开始工作。 ❌ B、D — 文章没提。

✍️ 环节四：完整句子训练（8分钟）

2e人称转换
Why did Aunt May never give up when people said nursing was difficult?
⚠️ 文章第二段最后一句是关键
✅ She never gave up because she wanted to help sick people get better.
💡 She wanted to help sick people get better. — 这就是她的动力。
2f人称转换
How long has the writer admired Aunt May?
⚠️ 最后一段有 since…
✅ The writer has admired her since he/she was a child.
💡 最后一段：I have admired her since I was a child. → 改人称：The writer has admired her since he was a child.

💎 环节五：一篇一得（5分钟）

活学 U4 语法：Present Perfect + for/since

for + 一段时间
has worked at QEH for 20 years
since + 时间点
has been a nurse since she was 22
have admired her since I was a child
✏️ 你来造两个句子：
① I have __________ for __________ .
② My __________ has __________ since __________ .

👨‍🏫 Ethan：今天怎么带多多？

  • 环节一：点词看意思。
  • 环节三：先让他自己读选项 A-D，自己排。然后问「为什么 C 不对？」——必须说出理由，不是「感觉」。这培养的是排除法的核心：每个划掉的选项都有理由。
  • 环节四：Q2f 的人称转换是关键——文章是「I」，答题要变成「the writer」或「he/she」。
  • 环节五：for/since 造句。如果他用「for January」→ 马上问「January 是一段时间还是时间点？」引导自我纠正。

📝 今天学到的

✅ 排除法三步：① 文章没提的 → ❌  ② 和文章相反的 → ❌  ③ 只有一半对的 → ❌
✅ for + 一段时间 / since + 时间点（再次巩固）
✅ 人称转换：I → the writer / he / she

第3篇：A Day at Ocean Park（海洋公园一日）

U5 Unforgettable experiences👤 人称转换难度：★★☆

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

roller coaster 过山车
frightened 害怕的
exciting 令人兴奋的
dolphin 海豚
souvenir 纪念品

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① How did the writer feel about the roller coaster?
② What did they buy at the end?

Last Saturday, my family and I went to Ocean Park. It was a sunny day. I felt very excited because I had never been to Ocean Park before.

First, we went on the roller coaster. It was really exciting, but I felt a little frightened when we went down the big drop. My brother was surprised because the ride was much faster than he expected. After the ride, I was tired but happy.

Then we watched the dolphin show. The dolphins were amazing. They jumped very high. There were so many people watching the show. Some children were clapping and laughing. The dolphins made everyone feel relaxed and joyful.

We had lunch at a restaurant near the aquarium. The food was delicious, but it cost so much money. After lunch, we visited the gift shop. My mum bought a dolphin keychain for me. It was the best day ever!

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防 — 人称转换是今天的核心！（10分钟）

👤 人称转换

技巧核心：文章用「I/my/we」，答题必须换成「He/She/They」！这是多多上次 Section H 全错的原因。

3a人称转换
Where did the writer go last Saturday?
⚠️ 文章：my family and I went to… → 答题：The writer / He / She went to…
✅ The writer went to Ocean Park last Saturday.
💡 文章第一句：my family and I went to Ocean Park → I 换成 the writer。千万别写「I went to Ocean Park」！
3b人称转换
How did the writer feel on the roller coaster?
⚠️ 两类感觉：excited（兴奋）和 frightened（害怕）都要提到
✅ The writer felt excited but also a little frightened on the roller coaster.
💡 第二段：It was exciting, but I felt a little frightened. → I → the writer。
⚠️ exciting = 过山车令人兴奋 / frightened = 作者感到害怕（注意 -ing 和 -ed 的区别！）
3c人称转换
What did the writer watch at Ocean Park?
⚠️ 不止一件事——roller coaster + dolphin show 都要写
✅ The writer watched the dolphin show at Ocean Park.
💡 第三段：we watched the dolphin show。如果概括也可以写多个活动，但 dolphin show 是「watched」的直接对象。
3d人称 + 语法
Why were there many people watching the dolphin show? Which phrase does the writer use to describe the crowd?
⚠️ 两个小问：① 为什么人多？② 作者用了哪个短语？
✅ The writer uses the phrase 'so many people' to describe the crowd.
💡 第三段：There were so many people — 「so many」是因为 people 可数！如果不可数呢？→ so much。

✍️ 环节四：完整句子训练（8分钟）

3e人称转换
What did the writer's mum buy at the gift shop?
⚠️ 文章：bought a dolphin keychain for me → 答题：for the writer
✅ The writer's mum bought a dolphin keychain for the writer at the gift shop.
💡 「for me」改为「for the writer」——这是最容易被扣分的地方！如果多多写「for him/her」也可以。
3f人称转换
Why did the writer say 'It was the best day ever'?
⚠️ 综合全文：roller coaster + dolphin + shopping = 快乐的一天
✅ The writer said it was the best day ever because he/she went on the roller coaster, watched the dolphin show, and got a dolphin keychain.
💡 这是综合题！要从全文找三个理由总结。考试中这种题2-3分。

💎 环节五：一篇一得（5分钟）

活学 U5 语法：-ed / -ing 形容词 + so many / so much

-ed = 感觉
I felt excited / frightened
I was tired / surprised
-ing = 令人…
The ride was exciting
The dolphins were amazing
✏️ 你来造两个句子：
① I felt __________ because __________ .
② There were so many __________ at __________ .

👨‍🏫 Ethan：今天怎么带多多？

  • 环节三前先强调：「今天所有题目，答案里如果出现 I / my / me —— 直接扣分！必须换成 the writer / he / she。」
  • Q3e 是重点：for me → for the writer。让他自己发现「me 要变成什么」。如果他写 for him — 告诉他「对！但你不知道是男是女，最好写 the writer。」
  • 环节五：用手势帮他记——指自己胸口 = -ed（我感觉）、指远处 = -ing（那东西令人…）

📝 今天学到的

✅ 人称转换铁律：I→the writer / she / he   my→the writer's / his / her   we→they   me→the writer
✅ -ed = 人的感觉 / -ing = 事物的特性
✅ 综合题：从全文找多个理由，用 because 串起来

第4篇：The MTR Rules（地铁守则）

U6 Being considerate🔄 同义替换难度：★★☆

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

considerate 体贴的
handrail 扶手
queue up 排队
disturb 打扰
block 挡住

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① What should you hold when you are on the MTR?
② What does the poster say about giving seats?

Last week, the MTR company put up a new poster in every station. The poster is called "Be Considerate on the MTR". It tells passengers what they ought to do and what they ought not to do.

First, the poster says passengers ought to hold the handrail when they are standing. If you do not hold the handrail, you may fall when the train stops suddenly. The poster also says you oughtn't to lean against the grab poles because other people need to hold them.

Second, the poster reminds passengers to give their seats to people in need, such as elderly people, pregnant women, and people with disabilities. You should let passengers exit first before you get on the train. Do not block the doors.

Third, the poster asks passengers to queue up on the platform. You ought not to jump the queue. Also, you should not take up extra seats with your bags or put your feet on the seats. These things make other passengers feel uncomfortable.

The poster has so many important messages. Everyone ought to follow these rules to make the MTR a better place for all.

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防 — 今天学同义替换！（10分钟）

🔄 同义替换

技巧核心：题目会用不同的词问文章里的内容。你要认出「换汤不换药」！
例：should → ought to  |  old people → elderly people  |  get on → board  |  don't → ought not to

题目可能写…
文章写的是…
should
ought to
should not
ought not to / oughtn't to
old people
elderly people
get on the train
board the train
wait in line
queue up
4a同义替换
What should passengers hold when standing on the MTR?
🔑 题目用 should → 文章用 ought to
✅ Passengers should (ought to) hold the handrail when they are standing.
💡 题目说 should，文章说 ought to hold the handrail —— 同一个意思！
4b同义替换
Why shouldn't passengers lean against the grab poles?
🔑 should not → oughtn't to
✅ They should not lean against the grab poles because other people need to hold them.
💡 文章：you oughtn't to lean against… because other people need to hold them。
4c同义替换
Who should passengers give their seats to?
🔑 give seats to → 找「who」
✅ Passengers should give their seats to people in need, such as elderly people, pregnant women, and people with disabilities.
💡 第三段。注意列举——这种题至少写两个例子才稳。
4d同义替换
What should you let passengers do before you board the train?
🔑 board = get on（同义替换！）
✅ You should let passengers exit first before you get on the train.
💡 题目说 board，文章说 get on —— 认出来了吗？
4e同义替换
What does the poster ask passengers to do on the platform?
🔑 文章第三段第一句
✅ The poster asks passengers to queue up on the platform.
💡 queue up 的同义是 wait in line / line up。
4f同义替换
What behaviour does the poster say passengers ought not to do when boarding?
🔑 ought not to → 找禁止事项
✅ Passengers ought not to jump the queue when boarding.
💡 同义：jump the queue = push in / cut in line。

✍️ 环节四：完整句子训练（8分钟）

4g综合
Why does the writer think everyone ought to follow the MTR rules?
✅ Everyone ought to follow the rules to make the MTR a better place for all.
💡 结尾段就是答案。to make… 表示目的。
4h综合
List three things that can make other passengers feel uncomfortable.
✅ Taking up extra seats with bags, putting feet on the seats, and jumping the queue can make passengers feel uncomfortable.
💡 第四段列举了三件。注意用 and 连接最后两项。

💎 环节五：一篇一得（5分钟）

活学 U6 语法：ought to / bare infinitive

ought to + V
ought to hold / ought to queue up / ought to follow
不带 to（let/make + sb + V）
let passengers exit first
make other passengers feel uncomfortable
✏️ 你来造句：
① At school, we ought to __________ .
② We ought not to __________ .

👨‍🏫 Ethan：今天怎么带多多？

  • 环节三前：先让他看「同义替换表」，考考他「should 在文章里可能写成什么？」→ ought to。
  • 每道题做完：问他「题目用的是哪个词？文章用的是哪个词？」让他自己发现同义替换的配对。
  • 环节五：bare infinitive（let sb exit）是重点。让他对比「let sb exit」和「ought to hold」——为什么一个有 to，一个没有？

📝 今天学到的

✅ 同义替换：should=ought to, should not=oughtn't to, elderly=old, board=get on, queue up=wait in line
✅ 看题要「翻译」成文章的语言
✅ bare infinitive：let/make + sb + V（不带to）

第5篇：My Grandma the Artist（画家奶奶）

U4+U5 综合🔮 推断题难度：★★★

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

artist 艺术家
exhibition 展览
talented 有天赋的
patient 有耐心的
encourage 鼓励

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① How long has Grandma been painting?
② What kind of person is Grandma?（答案不在字面上，要推断！）

My grandma, Mrs Chan, is 72 years old. She has been painting for more than 40 years. She started painting when she was in her thirties. At that time, she had three young children and very little free time. But she never stopped.

Grandma has so many paintings in her house. The walls are covered with her work. She paints mostly landscapes and flowers. Her favourite thing to paint is the sunset over the harbour. She says the colours change every day, so she never gets bored.

Last month, Grandma had her first art exhibition at a gallery in Central. So many people came to see her paintings. Some visitors said her paintings made them feel relaxed. A reporter from a local newspaper even interviewed her. Grandma felt very excited but also a little worried before the exhibition opened.

I have always admired my grandma. She has taught me that you are never too old to learn something new. When I have a problem, she always listens to me patiently. She never tells me what to do. Instead, she asks questions that help me find the answer myself. I think that is why everyone in our family loves her so much.

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防 — 今天学推断题！（10分钟）

🔮 推断题

技巧核心：有些答案文章没有直接说出来，但给了「线索」。你要像侦探一样把线索拼起来！
推断三步：① 找线索（文章说了什么）② 联想（这些线索说明什么）③ 验证（有没有其他线索支持？）

5a推断题
How old was Grandma when she started painting?
🔍 线索①：她72岁  线索②：画了超过40年  线索③：三十多岁开始的 → 推断！
✅ She started painting in her thirties. / She was in her thirties when she started painting.
💡 第二段直接说了 when she was in her thirties，但验证：72 - 40+ ≈ 30多 → 吻合！
5b推断题 ⭐
What kind of person is Grandma? Give TWO words to describe her and explain why.
🔍 文章没直接说「She is…」，但给了很多线索。找出来！
✅ Grandma is hard-working (because she painted even when she had three young children and little free time) and patient (because she listens to the writer patiently and never tells the writer what to do).
💡 还可以用的词：talented（画了40年+开展览）、kind（家人爱她）、determined（never stopped）。
🔑 推断题的关键：每个形容词都要从文章里找到支持的线索！
5c推断题
Why does Grandma never get bored of painting sunsets?
🔍 第二段最后一句。线索： colours change every day
✅ She never gets bored because the colours of the sunset change every day.
💡 这句是直接说的，但题目问「为什么」→ 从「colours change every day」推断出「每天都不一样，所以不无聊」。
5d推断题
How do you know that Grandma's exhibition was successful?
🔍 线索：很多线索散落在文章里——找出来拼！
✅ We know it was successful because (1) so many people came to see her paintings, (2) visitors said her paintings made them feel relaxed, and (3) a reporter from a newspaper interviewed her.
💡 文章没说「it was successful」，但三条线索加起来 → 可以推断成功！这就是推断题。

✍️ 环节四：完整句子训练（8分钟）

5e综合
What has Grandma taught the writer?
✅ Grandma has taught the writer that you are never too old to learn something new.
5f推断题
Why do you think everyone in the family loves Grandma?
✅ I think everyone loves her because she is patient, kind, and she helps people find their own answers instead of telling them what to do.
💡 最后一段说了她的做法（listen, never tell, ask questions）→ 推断出她受人尊敬的原因。

💎 环节五：一篇一得（5分钟）

现在完成
has been painting for 40 years

✏️ 造句：

② I think my __________ is __________ because __________ .

  • 推断题是难点，也是考试区分度所在。今天不要急，慢慢来。
  • Q5b：让多多先列出他在文章里找到的关于 Grandma 的每一条线索，然后问「这些线索加起来说明她是怎样的人？」
  • Q5d：问他「文章里有没有一句话说『the exhibition was successful』？」→ 没有。那你怎么知道成功？→ 引导他说出三条线索。

第6篇：A Scary Night in Sai Kung（西贡惊魂夜）

U5+U3 综合📋 顺序题难度：★★★

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

thunder 雷声
lightning 闪电
suddenly 突然
storm 暴风雨
relieved 松了一口气的

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① Why did the family go to Sai Kung?
② What happened when the storm came?

Last summer, my family and I went camping in Sai Kung. My father has always enjoyed outdoor activities. He has been a hiking lover since he was a teenager. We set up our tent near the beach. The weather was beautiful when we arrived.

First, we put up the tent together. It was the first time my brother and I had ever put up a tent, and we were very excited. Then my mum started a campfire. We roasted marshmallows and sang songs. Everything was perfect.

Suddenly, dark clouds covered the sky. A strong wind began to blow. Then we heard thunder. Lightning flashed across the sky. It was frightening. My little brother was frightened and started crying. The rain came down very hard. Our tent almost blew away.

We quickly packed our things and ran to the car. We were all wet and tired. When we finally got home, my mum said she had never felt so relieved. Although the trip was a bit disappointing, it was also an unforgettable experience. My dad said we will try camping again next year, but this time we will check the weather forecast first!

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防 — 今天学顺序题！（10分钟）

📋 顺序题

技巧核心：找「时间信号词」！文章用 First / Then / Suddenly / Finally 告诉你先后的。
序题不会做？回去扫每段开头的时间词！

👇 把下面的事件排成正确顺序（按先后点击）：
The storm came and it started raining
The family put up the tent
The family went camping in Sai Kung
Mum started a campfire and they roasted marshmallows
The family ran to the car and went home
✅ 正确顺序：C → B → D → A → E
（去露营 → 搭帐篷 → 生火烤棉花糖 → 暴风雨 → 跑回家）
💡 时间信号词：Paragraph 1: went camping → Paragraph 2: First, put up tent / Then, mum started campfire → Paragraph 3: Suddenly, storm → Paragraph 4: finally got home
6a定位
When did the family go camping in Sai Kung?
✅ The family went camping in Sai Kung last summer.
💡 第一句：Last summer…went camping。last summer = 过去时间 → 用一般过去式！
6b定位
How long has the father been a hiking lover?
✅ He has been a hiking lover since he was a teenager.
💡 有 since → 现在完成式！He has been…
6c定位
How did the writer's little brother feel during the storm?
✅ He was frightened and started crying.
💡 注意：frightened（人的感觉）vs frightening（暴风雨令人害怕）。第三段都有！

✍️ 环节四：完整句子训练（8分钟）

6d综合
Why did the writer's mum feel relieved when they got home?
✅ She felt relieved because they were finally safe at home after the scary storm.
💡 relieved 是 -ed（感觉），relieving 是用来说事物的——不要搞混！
6e推断题
What will the family do differently next time they go camping?
✅ Next time they will check the weather forecast first.
💡 最后一段爸爸的话就是答案。

💎 环节五：一篇一得（5分钟）

活学 时间信号词 + 时态选择

时间顺序信号词
First → Then → Suddenly → Finally
时态信号词
last summer → Simple Past
since + teenager → Present Perfect
✏️ 造句：用 First…Then…Finally… 写你上周末做了什么。

👨‍🏫 Ethan：今天怎么带多多？

  • 顺序题先用互动排序：让多多点击事件排顺序。排错了会有反馈。这是让他自己发现「时间信号词」的作用。
  • 排完后问：「你怎么知道 B 在 D 前面？」→ 引导他说出「因为文章有 First…Then…」
  • Q6c 的 -ed/-ing：the storm was frightening / my brother was frightened。让他对比——为什么一个是 -ing 一个是 -ed？

📝 今天学到的

✅ 顺序题找时间信号词：First / Then / Suddenly / Finally / when / after
✅ 叙述文的时态：过去事件 → Simple Past / 持续状态 → Present Perfect
✅ -ed vs -ing 再加巩固

第7篇：Helping at the Elderly Home（老人院义工）

U6+U4 综合🎯 综合实战难度：★★★

📋 环节一：生词速览（2分钟）

volunteer 义工/志愿者
elderly 年长的
community 社区
grateful 感激的
lonely 孤独的

👀 环节二：带问通读（5分钟）

带着这两个问题读：
① When did Chris start volunteering?
② Why does Chris think volunteering is important?

My name is Chris. I am a Secondary 3 student. I have been a volunteer at the Sunshine Elderly Home for two years. I started volunteering when I was in Secondary 1. At first, I was worried because I did not know how to talk to elderly people. But the staff there were very kind. They let me observe other volunteers first.

Every Saturday morning, I go to the elderly home. I help serve lunch and clean the tables. Sometimes I read the newspaper to the elderly residents. Many of them cannot read small words anymore. I also play chess with Mr Wong, who is 85 years old. Mr Wong has been living at the home since his wife passed away five years ago. He often says he feels lonely, but he always smiles when I come to visit.

I have learned so much from volunteering. I used to be shy, but now I am more confident. The elderly people have taught me many things about life. Mrs Lee, who is 90, told me that she has been a teacher for over 40 years. She still remembers all her students' names. She said, "You ought to be kind to everyone because you never know what they have been through."

I think everyone ought to try volunteering. It does not take so much time—just a few hours every week. But it can make someone feel less lonely. There are so many elderly people in our community who need company. Small acts of kindness can make a big difference.

🔍 环节三：逐段攻防 — 综合实战！（10分钟）

综合

今天是一周总结战！所有技巧混合：定位法 + 排除法 + 人称转换 + 同义替换 + 推断 + 顺序。看看多多能不能自己判断该用哪个。

7a定位 + 同义
How long has Chris been a volunteer?
🎒 技巧：定位法（找「volunteer」+「多久」）
✅ Chris has been a volunteer for two years.
💡 for two years — for + 时间段！
7bMC + 排除法
When did Chris start volunteering?
A. Two years ago
B. When he was in Secondary 1
C. When he was in Secondary 3
D. Five years ago
✅ B. When he was in Secondary 1
💡 文章：when I was in Secondary 1。 ❌ A — 是 for two years 不是 two years ago！ ❌ C — 他现在是中三。
7c推断题
Why does Chris read the newspaper to the elderly residents?
🔮 技巧：推断（文章没直接说「因为…」）
✅ He reads the newspaper to them because many of them cannot read small words anymore.
💡 第二段。anymore = 不再。注意这个字常出现在否定句中。
7d人称转换
What does Mrs Lee think people ought to do? Why?
👤 技巧：人称转换（Mrs Lee said "You ought to…" → 答题要改人称）
✅ Mrs Lee thinks people ought to be kind to everyone because you never know what they have been through.
💡 人称转换：You ought to → People ought to。have been through = 经历过。
7e推断题 ⭐
What does Chris mean by 'Small acts of kindness can make a big difference'? Give an example from the article.
✅ It means that even small things like reading the newspaper or playing chess with an elderly person can make them feel less lonely and happier. For example, Chris plays chess with Mr Wong, and Mr Wong always smiles when Chris visits.
💡 这是最难的题——既要理解抽象含义，又要从文章找例子。考试中这是3分题。

✍️ 环节四：完整句子训练（8分钟）

7f综合
How has Chris changed since he started volunteering?
✅ Chris used to be shy, but now he is more confident.
💡 第三段：used to be shy → now more confident。used to = 过去常常（现在已经不是了）。
7g综合
Why does Chris think everyone ought to try volunteering? Give at least two reasons.
✅ Chris thinks everyone ought to try volunteering because (1) it does not take much time, (2) it can make elderly people feel less lonely, and (3) there are many elderly people in the community who need company.
