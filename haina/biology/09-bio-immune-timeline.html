0 hr — Infection Begins 感染開始
🦠 Pathogen Invades the Body病原體入侵人體
Bacteria or virus enters through a cut, air, or food. The immune system is alerted. 點擊展開詳情

Pathogens (disease-causing microorganisms such as bacteria, viruses, fungi) enter the body through various routes — skin cuts, respiratory tract, digestive system, or body fluids.

病原體（致病微生物，如細菌、病毒、真菌）通過各種途徑進入人體——皮膚傷口、呼吸道、消化系統或體液。

Once inside, they begin to multiply → this triggers the body’s immune response.

一旦進入，它們開始繁殖 → 觸發身體的免疫反應。

1st Line of Defence 第一道防線
0 hr — First Line of Defence 第一道防線
🏰 Physical & Chemical Barriers物理及化學屏障
Skin, mucous membranes, tears, stomach acid — the body’s outermost shield. 點擊展開詳情

The first line of defence is non-specific — it blocks all pathogens equally:

第一道防線是非特異性的——它對所有病原體一視同仁地阻擋：

🦴 Skin — physical barrier; waterproof, slightly acidic → kills some bacteria
👃 Mucous membrane — traps pathogens in sticky mucus
👁️ Tears — contain lysozyme (enzyme) that destroys bacterial cell walls
🍽️ Stomach acid (HCl) — kills most pathogens in food
💨 Cilia in airways — sweep mucus + trapped pathogens upward

🦴 皮膚——物理屏障；防水、微酸性 → 殺死部分細菌 | 👃 黏膜——用黏稠的黏液捕捉病原體 | 👁️ 眼淚——含溶菌酶，破壞細菌細胞壁 | 🍽️ 胃酸（HCl）——殺死食物中大多數病原體 | 💨 氣管纖毛——將黏液和被捕捉的病原體向上掃出

1st Line of Defence
0–6 hr — Second Line of Defence 第二道防線
⚔️ Phagocytes Engulf Pathogens吞噬細胞吞噬病原體
White blood cells that "eat" any foreign invader — non-specific, fast response. 點擊展開詳情

When pathogens breach the first line, phagocytes (a type of white blood cell) arrive at the infection site:

當病原體突破第一道防線，吞噬細胞（一種白血球）到達感染部位：

1. Phagocyte engulfs the pathogen (phagocytosis)
2. Digests it using enzymes inside the phagocyte
3. This is still non-specific — any pathogen gets eaten
4. Inflammation (redness, swelling, heat, pain) occurs — increased blood flow brings more phagocytes

1. 吞噬細胞吞入病原體（吞噬作用）| 2. 用吞噬細胞內的酶消化它 | 3. 仍然是非特異性的——任何病原體都會被吃掉 | 4. 發生發炎反應（紅、腫、熱、痛）——增加血流帶來更多吞噬細胞

Blood clotting also occurs at wounds — platelets + fibrin form a clot to seal the wound and prevent more pathogens from entering.

傷口處也發生凝血——血小板 + 纖維蛋白形成血塊封住傷口，阻止更多病原體進入。

2nd Line of Defence
3–7 days — Third Line of Defence 第三道防線
🎯 Lymphocytes Produce Specific Antibodies淋巴細胞產生特異性抗體
The smart weapon: each antibody matches ONE specific antigen. 點擊展開詳情

The third line of defence is specific — it targets ONE particular pathogen:

第三道防線是特異性的——它針對一種特定病原體：

🔑 Lymphocytes (a type of WBC) recognise the antigen (a specific molecule on the pathogen’s surface).

🔑 淋巴細胞（一種白血球）識別抗原（病原體表面的特定分子）。

🔒 Lymphocytes produce antibodies that fit the antigen like a lock and key. Each antibody is specific to ONE antigen.

🔒 淋巴細胞產生抗體，與抗原像鎖和鑰匙一樣匹配。每種抗體只針對一種抗原。

⚡ Antibodies bind to antigens → neutralise the pathogen or mark it for destruction by phagocytes.

⚡ 抗體與抗原結合 → 中和病原體，或標記它讓吞噬細胞消滅。

7–14 days — Primary Response 初次反應
📈 Antibody Level Peaks & Pathogen Cleared抗體水平達峰，病原體被清除
Slow start, gradual rise — this is the primary immune response. 點擊展開詳情 + 圖表

The primary response (first exposure to a pathogen):

初次反應（第一次接觸某病原體）：

🐢 Slow — takes several days for lymphocytes to recognise the antigen and produce antibodies
📉 Low peak — antibody concentration rises gradually
🤒 You may feel sick during this time because the pathogen is multiplying while the immune system catches up

🐢 慢——淋巴細胞需要數天才能識別抗原並產生抗體 | 📉 峰值低——抗體濃度逐漸上升 | 🤒 在此期間你可能會生病，因為病原體在繁殖，而免疫系統還在追趕

14 days → Years — Memory Cells Remain 記憶細胞留存
🧠 Memory Cells — The Immune System’s Secret Weapon記憶細胞——免疫系統的秘密武器
After recovery, some lymphocytes become memory cells — they "remember" the pathogen for years. 點擊展開詳情

After the pathogen is cleared, most lymphocytes die off — but some become memory cells:

病原體被清除後，大多數淋巴細胞會死亡——但有些變成記憶細胞：

🧠 Memory cells stay in the body for years or a lifetime
🔍 They "remember" the specific antigen
⚡ If the same pathogen invades again → memory cells rapidly produce large amounts of specific antibodies
🛡️ This is why you usually only get some diseases once (e.g. chickenpox)

🧠 記憶細胞在體內留存數年甚至終身 | 🔍 它們「記得」特定抗原 | ⚡ 如果同一病原體再次入侵 → 記憶細胞快速產生大量特異性抗體 | 🛡️ 這就是為什麼某些疾病通常只會感染一次（如：水痘）

This is the basis of vaccination 💉 — we create memory cells WITHOUT getting the actual disease.

這就是疫苗接種的基礎 💉——我們在不真正感染疾病的情況下建立記憶細胞。

3rd Line of Defence · Memory
Months later — Secondary Response 二次反應
⚡ Faster & Stronger — The Memory Cells at Work更快更強——記憶細胞的作用
Same pathogen again → antibodies surge within hours, not days. 點擊展開圖表對比

The secondary response (second exposure to the same pathogen) is dramatically different:

二次反應（再次接觸同一病原體）與初次截然不同：

🐢 Primary Response 初次反應

Slow onset (several days)

起效慢（數天）

Low antibody peak

抗體峰值低

You feel sick 🤒

你會生病

⚡ Secondary Response 二次反應

Fast onset (hours)

起效快（數小時）

High antibody peak

抗體峰值高

You may NOT feel sick 🛡️

你可能不生病

Why? Memory cells from the first infection are ready → they rapidly divide and produce antibodies → the pathogen is destroyed before it can multiply enough to cause symptoms.

為什麼？第一次感染留下的記憶細胞已經準備就緒 → 它們快速分裂並產生抗體 → 病原體在繁殖到足以引起症狀之前就被消滅。

💉 Vaccination — Artificial Active Immunity
💉 How Vaccines Work 疫苗原理人工主動免疫
Training the immune system without getting sick. 點擊展開詳情

Vaccination creates immunity without suffering from the actual disease:

疫苗接種在不真正患病的情況下建立免疫力：

💉 1. A vaccine contains dead or weakened pathogens (or just the antigen). 💉 1. 疫苗含有已死或弱化的病原體（或僅抗原）。
🔍 2. Lymphocytes recognise the antigen → produce specific antibodies. 🔍 2. 淋巴細胞識別抗原 → 產生特異性抗體。
🧠 3. Memory cells are formed — they stay in the body for years. 🧠 3. 形成記憶細胞——在體內留存多年。
⚡ 4. If the real pathogen invades later → memory cells trigger a fast, strong secondary response → you don’t get sick. ⚡ 4. 如果真正的病原體日後入侵 → 記憶細胞觸發快速、強烈的二次反應 → 你不會生病。

Active immunity 主動免疫： The body makes its own antibodies after exposure to an antigen (natural infection OR vaccination). Long-lasting.
Passive immunity 被動免疫： The body receives ready-made antibodies from another source (e.g. mother’s breast milk, antibody injection). Short-term, no memory cells formed.

主動免疫：身體在接觸抗原後自己製造抗體（自然感染或疫苗接種）。持久有效。 | 被動免疫：身體接收現成抗體（如：母乳、抗體注射）。短期有效，無記憶細胞形成。
