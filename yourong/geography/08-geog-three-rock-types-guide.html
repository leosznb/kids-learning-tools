Rock Type 1: Igneous — Extrusive (Volcanic) 噴出火成岩

EXTRUSIVE Formation Process 形成過程
1
Magma rises from deep underground toward the surface.
岩漿從地下深處向地表上升。
2
Volcanic eruption — magma breaks through the Earth's crust.
火山爆發 — 岩漿衝破地殼。
3
Lava and volcanic ash are ejected above the ground surface.
熔岩與火山灰噴出地表。
4
Lava and ash cool rapidly in contact with air and water.
熔岩與火山灰接觸空氣和水後快速冷卻。
5
Rock solidifies with fine / small crystals (fast cooling leaves no time for large crystals to grow).
岩石凝固形成細小晶體（快速冷卻沒有足夠時間讓大晶體生長）。
Crystal size
Fine / small crystals
細小晶體
Structure
No layers; hexagonal columnar jointing
無層理；六角形柱狀節理
Resistance
More resistant to weathering
較抗風化
Examples in HK
Volcanic tuff, basalt — Sai Kung, High Island
火山凝灰岩、玄武岩 — 西貢、萬宜水庫
Landforms
High hills, hexagonal columns, sea arches, sea stacks
高山、六角柱、海蝕拱、海蝕柱
Why hexagonal columns?
Lava contracts evenly as it cools, cracks form at 120° angles, producing hexagons
熔岩冷卻時均勻收縮，裂縫以120度角形成，產生六角柱

Rock Type 2: Igneous — Intrusive 侵入火成岩

INTRUSIVE Formation Process 形成過程
1
Magma rises but does NOT reach the surface.
岩漿上升但未到達地表。
2
Magma enters cracks and spaces below the ground surface (intrudes into existing rock layers).
岩漿進入地表以下的裂隙和空間（侵入現有岩層）。
3
Magma cools very slowly underground (surrounded by warm rock, heat escapes gradually).
岩漿在地下非常緩慢地冷卻（被溫暖的岩石包圍，熱量逐漸散失）。
4
Rock solidifies with coarse / large crystals (slow cooling gives time for large crystals to grow).
岩石凝固形成粗大晶體（緩慢冷卻給予大晶體生長的時間）。
Crystal size
Coarse / large crystals
粗大晶體
Structure
No layers; large mineral grains visible
無層理；肉眼可見粗大礦物顆粒
Resistance
Highly resistant to weathering
高度抗風化
Examples in HK
Granite — Ma On Shan, common in HK
花崗岩 — 馬鞍山、香港常見
Landforms
High hills, rounded boulders
高山、圓形巨石
The "Cooling Speed Story" 冷卻速度故事 — the single most important concept for igneous rocks.
🌋
Extrusive 噴出
Above ground. Fast cooling (seconds to hours). Fine crystals.
地表。快速冷卻（數秒至數小時）。細小晶體。
比喻：Hot soup spilled on a cold table — surface cools instantly. 熱湯灑在冷桌上 — 表面瞬間冷卻。
🪨
Intrusive 侵入
Below ground. Very slow cooling (thousands to millions of years). Large crystals.
地下。極慢冷卻（數千至數百萬年）。粗大晶體。
比喻：Hot soup kept in a thermos — cools very slowly. 熱湯放在保溫壺中 — 冷卻極慢。

Rock Type 3: Sedimentary 沉積岩

SEDIMENTARY Formation Process — Six-Step Method (WET DCC)
1
Weathering 風化: Rocks on the surface are broken down by weathering (rain, wind, temperature changes).
地表岩石被風化作用（雨水、風、溫度變化）分解。
2
Erosion 侵蝕: Weathered rock fragments are loosened and removed.
風化後的岩石碎片被鬆動並移走。
3
Transport 搬運: Rock fragments are transported by water, wind, or ice to a new location (usually low-lying areas like river mouths or seabeds).
岩石碎片被水、風或冰搬運至新位置（通常為低窪地區如河口或海底）。
4
Deposition 沉積: Transported materials are deposited when the energy of water / wind / ice decreases.
當水/風/冰的能量減弱時，搬運物質沉積下來。
5
Compaction 壓實: More layers accumulate on top — weight increases — lower layers are compressed (compacted).
更多岩層堆積在上層 — 重量增加 — 下層被壓縮（壓實）。
6
Cementation 膠結: Minerals dissolved in water crystallise between sediment grains, binding them together into solid rock.
水中溶解的礦物在沉積顆粒之間結晶，將它們膠結成堅硬岩石。
Mnemonic 記憶口訣: WET DCC — Weather, Erode, Transport, Deposit, Compact, Cement
Crystal size
No crystals; contains sediments / fossils
無晶體，含沉積物或化石
Structure
Layered / stratified (youngest on top)
層理結構（最年輕的在最上）
Why fossils?
Dead organisms buried in sediment are preserved
埋於沉積物中的生物遺骸被保存為化石
Resistance
Less resistant to weathering
較易風化
Examples in HK
Sandstone, mudstone, shale — NE NT, Ping Chau, Port Island
砂岩、泥岩、頁岩 — 新界東北、平洲、赤洲
Landforms
Low hills, wave-cut platforms, fold structures
矮山、海蝕平台、褶曲構造

Rock Type 4: Metamorphic 變質岩

METAMORPHIC Formation Process 形成過程
1
An existing rock (igneous, sedimentary, or even another metamorphic rock) is subjected to extreme heat and pressure deep underground.
現有岩石（火成岩、沉積岩或甚至另一種變質岩）在地下深處承受極端高溫和高壓。
2
The heat and pressure change the mineral composition and texture of the rock without melting it completely.
高溫和高壓改變岩石的礦物成分和質地，但並未完全熔化。
3
New minerals form and crystals may rearrange into bands or layers (foliated texture).
新礦物形成，晶體可能重新排列成帶狀或層狀（葉理）。
The "No Melting Rule" 不熔化法則: The key difference between metamorphic and igneous: metamorphic rocks form WITHOUT melting. The original rock is changed by heat and pressure, but it never becomes liquid magma.
變質岩與火成岩的關鍵區別：變質岩形成時不熔化。原始岩石被高溫高壓改變，但從未變成液態岩漿。
Crystal size
Crystals rearranged into bands / layers
晶體重新排列成帶狀/層狀
Structure
Foliated (banded) or non-foliated
葉理（帶狀）或非葉理
Foliated vs Non-foliated
Foliated = minerals aligned under directional pressure; Non-foliated = no banding, uniform pressure
葉理=定向壓力下礦物排列成帶；非葉理=均勻壓力下無帶狀
Resistance
Generally very resistant
通常非常抗風化
Examples
Marble (from limestone), Slate (from shale), Schist, Gneiss
大理岩（由石灰岩變質）、板岩（由頁岩變質）、片岩、片麻岩
Landforms
Rugged terrain, resistant ridges
崎嶇地形、抗風化山脊

Key Rule: Always link formation to characteristics to landform

Bad answer 差答案: "Sai Kung has volcanic tuff. It has fine crystals."

Good answer 好答案: "Sai Kung has igneous extrusive rock (volcanic tuff). It formed above the ground surface when lava cooled rapidly in contact with air, producing fine crystals and no layers. Because it is more resistant to weathering, it forms high hills such as those at High Island."

Self-Test: Identify the Rock Type 自測：判斷岩類

Click each question to reveal the answer. 點擊題目查看答案。

Q1: This rock has coarse crystals visible to the naked eye. It formed deep underground and is very common in Hong Kong. It creates high hills.
Answer

Igneous — Intrusive (Granite) 侵入火成岩（花崗岩）

Key clues: coarse crystals (slow cooling underground), common in HK, high hills. 關鍵線索：粗晶體（地下緩慢冷卻）、香港常見、高山。

Q2: This rock has horizontal layers. It contains fossils. It is found at Ping Chau and Port Island. It weathers easily and forms low hills.
Answer

Sedimentary (Sandstone / Mudstone / Shale) 沉積岩（砂岩/泥岩/頁岩）

Key clues: layered structure, fossils, Ping Chau / Port Island, less resistant, low hills. 關鍵線索：層理結構、化石、平洲/赤洲、易風化、矮山。

Q3: This rock has fine crystals and hexagonal columnar joints. It is found at High Island. It formed from lava that cooled quickly above the ground.
Answer

Igneous — Extrusive (Volcanic tuff) 噴出火成岩（火山凝灰岩）

Key clues: fine crystals, hexagonal columns, High Island, rapid cooling above ground. 關鍵線索：細晶體、六角柱、萬宜水庫、地表快速冷卻。

Q4: This rock has bands of minerals aligned in parallel layers. It formed from limestone that was subjected to heat and pressure deep underground. It did not melt.
Answer

Metamorphic (Marble) 變質岩（大理岩）

Key clues: foliated / banded structure, formed from existing rock (limestone), heat + pressure, no melting. 關鍵線索：葉理/帶狀結構、由石灰岩變質、高溫高壓、不熔化。

Exam Traps to Avoid 考試陷阱

⚠️
Confusing "no crystals" with "no layers"
Sedimentary has no crystals BUT has layers. Igneous has crystals BUT no layers. 沉積岩無晶體但有層理；火成岩有晶體但無層理。
Memorise separately: crystal size ≠ structure. 分開記憶：晶體大小 ≠ 結構。
⚠️
Saying all igneous rocks have large crystals
Only intrusive igneous has large crystals. Extrusive has fine crystals. 只有侵入火成岩有粗大晶體；噴出火成岩是細小晶體。
Always specify "intrusive" or "extrusive". 必須標明「侵入」或「噴出」。
⚠️
Forgetting to mention cooling speed
Cooling speed is WHY crystal size differs. Without it, the explanation is incomplete. 冷卻速度是晶體大小的原因，不解釋就不完整。
Always include: "rapid cooling produces fine crystals" or "slow cooling produces large crystals".
⚠️
Saying sedimentary rocks "melt" to form
Sedimentary forms from sediments, not from melting. Only igneous forms from cooled magma/lava. 沉積岩來自沉積物而非熔化。
Remember WET DCC: Weather to Erode to Transport to Deposit to Compact to Cement.
⚠️
Calling metamorphic rocks "melted"
Metamorphic rocks form under heat and pressure WITHOUT melting. 變質岩不熔化。
Remember the "No Melting Rule". 記住「不熔化法則」。
⚠️
Only listing features without linking
Marking scheme rewards LINKED explanations (cause to effect). 只列特徵不連貫。
Always say: "Because X, therefore Y, resulting in Z". 永遠使用「因為X，所以Y，導致Z」。
