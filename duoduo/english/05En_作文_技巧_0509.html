✍️ 多多 English Writing — 英文作文技巧

英作 30 分鐘　｜　P5 呈分試

▶⏱ Time Plan（30分鐘怎麼用）

StageTimeWhat to do
① Plan3 minRead the question carefully. Underline key words. Decide: story / letter / description?
② Write20 minFollow your plan. Write complete sentences. Don't stop to fix grammar.
③ Check7 minCheck: tense, capital letters, full stops, spelling, word count (~80-100 words)
⚠️ 香港考試要寫完整句子！ 不要只寫關鍵詞。例如：不要寫 "went park happy"，要寫 "I went to the park and I felt very happy."
▶📐 The 3-Part Structure（三段式萬能框架）
Every composition has 3 parts:
Beginning (1-2 sentences) → Introduce the topic
Middle (4-5 sentences) → Tell the story / give details
Ending (1-2 sentences) → Finish with a feeling or lesson
📝 框架範例 (Story)
Beginning: Last Sunday, I went to the park with my family.
Middle: We had a picnic there. I played football with my brother. Suddenly, it started to rain. We ran to a shelter. Although we got wet, we laughed a lot.
Ending: It was an unforgettable day. I hope we can go again next time.
▶🔤 Sentence Starters（必背開頭句式）
UseSentence StarterExample
開頭Last week / Yesterday / One day, ...Last week, something exciting happened.
開頭I will never forget the day when...I will never forget the day when I met my best friend.
轉折However, / But then, / Suddenly,Suddenly, I heard a loud noise.
因果Because of this, / As a result,As a result, I learned an important lesson.
感受I felt + happy / excited / nervous / scaredI felt very proud of myself.
結尾In the end, / Finally,In the end, everything turned out well.
結尾I learned that ...I learned that we should never give up.
結尾I hope / I will always rememberI will always remember this special day.
▶🔗 Linking Words（連接詞 — 讓作文流暢）
First, ... Next, ... Then, ... After that, ... Finally, ...
用來說明先後次序
and　/　but　/　so　/　because　/　or
最基本的連接詞，每篇至少用 3 次
also　/　too　/　as well
補充更多信息（放在句尾或句中）
for example, ...　/　such as ...

▶📄 Sample Composition（範文 — 背結構）
📝 Topic: A Memorable Day （難忘的一天）~85 words
Last Saturday, I went to the beach with my family. The weather was sunny and warm. First, we played in the sand and built a big sandcastle. Then, I swam in the sea with my brother. The water was cool and refreshing. Suddenly, I saw a crab walking on the sand! I felt very excited. After that, we had a picnic under a tree. We ate sandwiches and drank juice. Finally, we watched the sunset together. It was a wonderful day. I will always remember it.
📝 Topic: A Person I Admire （我敬佩的人）~80 words
The person I admire most is my mother. She is kind and hardworking. Every day, she wakes up early to prepare breakfast for our family. She also helps me with my homework when I have problems. I remember one time when I was sick, she stayed with me the whole night. I felt very grateful. Because of her, I have learned to be caring and responsible. I hope I can be like her one day.
