Q1: Why can't solid NaCl conduct, but aqueous NaCl can?為什麼固體 NaCl 不導電，溶於水就能導電？

🧊
Solid NaCl固體 NaCl
Na⁺ and Cl⁻ locked in lattice被鎖在晶格裡 They can only vibrate, cannot move freely只能原地振動，不能自由移動

No mobile ions沒有自由移動的離子
→ Does NOT conduct ❌不導電
💧
NaCl aqueous solutionNaCl 水溶液
Water molecules pull ions out of the lattice水分子把離子從晶格拉出來 Ions are free to move離子可以自由游動

Mobile ions present有自由移動的離子
→ Conducts electricity ✅導電

Conduction requires freely moving charged particles

導電需要「可以自由移動的帶電粒子」

🔵 Conductor (metal) → delocalized electrons carry the current → conducts in both solid and liquid states
金屬導體靠自由電子移動 → 固體、液體都能導電
🟠 Electrolyte → mobile ions carry the current → only conducts when molten or in aqueous solution
電解質靠自由離子移動 → 只有熔融或溶於水時才導電

📌 In one sentence

Solid NaCl: ions present but cannot move → no conduction.
Aqueous NaCl: ions free to move → conducts.
It's not about having ions — it's about whether they can move.

固體 NaCl：離子在，但動不了 → 不導電。溶於水：離子自由了 → 導電。
不是「有沒有離子」，是「離子能不能動」。

The secret: it's not about having a metal — it's about whether ions are produced when dissolved

HCl(g) + H₂O(l) → H₃O⁺(aq) + Cl⁻(aq)

Water splits HCl molecules → produces H⁺ and Cl⁻ ions
水把 HCl 分子拆開 → 產生 H⁺ 和 Cl⁻ 離子

Ultimate test for exams終極判斷法

Q: Is this substance an electrolyte?

① Solid? → Definitely NOT — electrolytes do NOT conduct as solids
固體 → 一定不是，electrolyte 在固體時不導電

② Molten / aqueous? → Check for mobile ions
熔融 / 水溶液 → 看有沒有自由離子
· Ionic compound (metal + non-metal) → has ions → electrolyte ✅
· Acid dissolved in water → dissociates to give H⁺ → electrolyte ✅
· Pure covalent (alcohol, oil, distilled water) → no ions → NOT ❌
離子化合物 → 有 · 酸溶於水 → 有 · 純共價分子 → 沒有

③ Chemical decomposition during conduction?
→ Yes → electrolyte ✅
→ No (e.g. metals) → conductor only, NOT an electrolyte
導電時有化學分解？有 → electrolyte · 沒有（如金屬）→ conductor only

Master these three questions, and you've mastered electrolytes.
記住這三個問題的答案，Electrolytes 這一課就通了。 NaCl solid vs aqueous = can ions move · HCl = acid dissociates · Decomposed = compound broken into elements
NaCl 固體 vs 水溶液 = 離子能不能動 · HCl = 酸解離 · Decomposed = 化合物被電拆回元素
