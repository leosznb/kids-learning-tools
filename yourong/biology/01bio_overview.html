#1 Nutrition & Food Substances

📖 Textbook P.1–52📓 Workbook P.1–18📝 Supp Notes P.2–9⏱ 估佔分 ~25%

· 6類食物物質功能默寫（Carbs/Proteins/Lipids/Vitamins/Minerals/Fibre/Water）

· Reducing vs Non-reducing sugar區分（Benedict's測不到蔗糖）
· 🆕 Carbs分類：Monosaccharide(Glucose/Fructose/Galactose)、Disaccharide(Maltose/Sucrose/Lactose)、Polysaccharide(Starch/Glycogen/Cellulose)

· 🆕 Protein結構：Amino acid → Dipeptide → Polypeptide → Protein
· BMI計算 + Food Pyramid + 均衡飲食 + 能量值(Lipid最高~38kJ/g > Protein≈Carbs~17 > Water=0)
⚠️ 最常錯：Benedict's不加熱=無效測試；DCPIP脫色是還原反應；Glucose test paper只測glucose不測其他還原糖；Sucrose是Non-reducing

#2 Human Digestive System

📖 Textbook P.53–58📓 Workbook P.19–23📝 Supp Notes P.10–12⏱ 估佔分 ~25%
考核要點：
· 消化系統完整路徑：Oral cavity → Oesophagus(蠕動) → Stomach(胃液HCl+胃蛋白酶) → Duodenum(胰液+膽汁) → Ileum(腸液+Villi吸收) → Colon(吸水) → Rectum(儲存排遺)
· 各部位消化液/酶/底物/產物——必考填空配對
· Bile：肝產、膽囊儲、無酶、乳化脂肪=物理消化——每年必考3分題
· Villi 4大適應特徵：大表面積/薄壁(1 cell thick)/豐富微血管/Lacteal
· 🆕 Blood vs Lymph transport：Blood→Glucose/Amino acids/Minerals/Vit C；Lymph(Lacteal)→Fatty acids/Glycerol/Fat-soluble vitamins(A,D,E,K)
· 🆕 Human Nutrition 5步驟：Ingestion→Digestion→Absorption→Assimilation→Egestion
· Dietary Fibre兩大功能(吸水增體積防便秘+吸毒素降腸癌風險) ⚠️不被消化
⚠️ 最常錯：Pancreatic juice ≠ Bile；Bile是物理消化不是化學；Fibre不被消化；Blood vs Lymph吸收路徑搞反；Assimilation常被遺漏

#3 Enzymes

📖 Textbook P.42–68（貫穿）📝 Supp Notes P.5–7📎 12.2a PPT⏱ 估佔分 ~20%
考核要點：
· 酶五大特性：Biological catalyst / Specific(lock-and-key) / Reusable / Protein / Lower activation energy
· Lock-and-key hypothesis：Active site + Substrate → ES complex → Product（酶不變可重用）
· 溫度曲線：最適溫度(~37°C)→高溫Denaturation(不可逆)→低溫暫時抑制(可逆)
· pH影響：唾液澱粉酶~pH7 / 胃蛋白酶~pH2 / 脂肪酶~pH8
· Denaturation = active site形狀永久改變，substrate無法結合
· 🆕 Competitive Inhibitor：類似substrate形狀→競爭active site→可逆(Vmax不變)
· 🆕 Non-competitive Inhibitor：結合allosteric site→改變酶形狀→常不可逆(Vmax降低)
· 實驗設計變量：IV(改變的因素) / DV(測量的結果) / CV(保持不變)→必考填空
· 🆕 Graph Plotting：尺子畫軸、標註軸名+單位、合適比例、×標點、best-fit line
⚠️ 最常錯：Denaturation不可逆vs低溫可逆；IV/DV/CV分不清；Competitive vs Non-competitive曲線搞反；作圖不標單位
🛠️ #1 閃卡#4 酶活性指揮中心#5 概念對決

🎯Biology 答題技巧全攻略（海納·有容專區）

以下技巧基於荃官歷年Bio試卷扣分點分析。海納和有容常「答不到點子上」被扣分——不是不懂，是沒有用考試要的語言和結構來答。這部分就是你們的得分轉化器。

⚡ 黃金法則：Biology Structure 題答題三步曲

荃官Structure題的評分邏輯是 「寫對關鍵詞 → 給分；缺少關鍵詞 → 零分」。以下三步曲適配所有≥3分的簡答/論述題：

步驟做什麼Bio實例
Step 1
指出原理/定義
(1–2分)		用完整英文句寫出涉及的biological concept或definition"Denaturation is the permanent change in the shape of the active site of an enzyme, causing the substrate to be unable to bind."
Step 2
解釋因果/機制
(1–2分)		用 "This is because..." 或 "As a result..." 連接因果"This is because high temperature breaks the bonds that maintain the specific 3D shape. As a result, the enzyme can no longer catalyse the reaction."
Step 3
聯繫情境/總結
(1分)		回到題目給的具體情境，說出對 data / graph / case 的判斷"In this experiment, the rate of reaction decreased significantly after 40°C, indicating that salivary amylase was denatured."

📐 分題型答題框架

🅰️ 數據/圖表分析題（Data/Graph Interpretation）· 佔分 3–5

最常見扣分：只描述數字變化但沒有比較、沒有單位、沒有引用具體數據。

正確答法四要素：
① 趨勢詞：increase / decrease / remain constant / reach a peak / drop sharply
② 比較詞：higher than / lower than / twice as much as / compared to
③ 數字+單位：from 25°C to 40°C / increased by 50% / at pH 7
④ 生物學解釋：This shows that... / This is because...

示例——血糖曲線題（3分）：

❌ 錯：The blood glucose level went up and then down. Person B's level is higher.
✅ 對：After the meal, the blood glucose level of Person A increased from 5 mmol/L to a peak of 9 mmol/L at 30 minutes, then returned to normal by 120 minutes. Person B's blood glucose level remained above 11 mmol/L even after 120 minutes, which is significantly higher than Person A. This suggests that Person B may have diabetes because his body cannot regulate blood glucose effectively.

🅱️ 實驗設計題（Experimental Design）· 佔分 4–6

最常見扣分：IV / DV / CV分不清；控制變量寫不完整；沒有寫control set-up。

必答清單：
· Independent Variable (IV)：The factor that is changed by the experimenter
· Dependent Variable (DV)：The factor that is measured as the result
· Controlled Variables (CV)：Factors kept constant to ensure a fair test
· Control set-up：A set-up without the IV, used for comparison

示例——唾液澱粉酶實驗（4分）：

✅ IV: Temperature of the water bath (e.g. 10°C, 20°C, 30°C, 40°C, 50°C)
✅ DV: Time taken for the iodine test to show no blue-black colour (or the rate of starch digestion)
✅ CV: Volume of starch solution, volume of salivary amylase, pH, type of starch used
✅ Control: A tube containing boiled salivary amylase (denatured enzyme) under the same conditions

🅲️ 流程/機制描述題（Process Description）· 佔分 4–6

最常見扣分：步驟漏掉中間環節、缺少連接詞、沒有按順序。

三步驗證法——寫完後自問：
① 起因寫了嗎？（What triggers the process?）
② 每個中間步驟都寫了嗎？（Are all intermediate steps included?）
③ 結果寫了嗎？（What is the final outcome?）

示例——蛀牙機制（4分）：

✅ ① Bacteria in the mouth feed on sugar remaining on teeth. → ② The bacteria produce acid as a waste product. → ③ The acid dissolves the enamel, the hardest outer layer of the tooth. → ④ Once the enamel is broken, the acid reaches the dentine, causing a cavity. → ⑤ When the cavity reaches the pulp cavity, it irritates the nerve, causing toothache.

🅳️ 比較/對比題（Comparison）· 佔分 3–4

最常見扣分：只寫了一方的特徵，沒有同時對比另一方；用詞不對（same/different/both/whereas）。

對比題必用句型：
· "X is... while/whereas Y is..."
· "Unlike X, Y..."
· "Both X and Y are... but X differs in that..."
· "X has... compared to Y, which has..."

示例——Primary vs Secondary Immune Response（3分）：

✅ The secondary response produces antibodies much faster than the primary response. The lag time in the secondary response is shorter. The peak antibody concentration in the secondary response is significantly higher. This is because memory cells are produced during the primary response and remain in the body, ready to produce antibodies rapidly upon re-exposure to the same antigen.

🅴️ 案例診斷題（Case Diagnosis）· 佔分 3–4

最常見扣分：診斷對了但沒寫理由；只寫了食物建議但沒解釋為什麼那個食物有效。

診斷題三步：
① Diagnosis：Identify the disease/condition from symptoms
② Cause：State the deficiency or risk factor
③ Solution：Recommend food / action + explain why it works

而是"food is digested / broken down"

9空著不寫寫關鍵詞碰分——即使不完全確定，寫相關term也有機會拿1–2分不確定是不是Denaturation→照寫"the enzyme's active site changed shape"也可能給分
10沒檢查就交卷最後5分鐘：查關鍵詞拼寫+mark allocation對應重點查：denaturation / villi / Benedict's / salivary amylase / antibody
