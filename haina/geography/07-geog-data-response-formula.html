1 Trend Description 趨勢描述 ▼

Pattern A — Increase / Decrease 上升 / 下降

The [variable] increased / decreased from [X] in [year] to [Y] in [year], with a change of [Z].

[變量] 從 [年份] 的 [X] 上升 / 下降至 [年份] 的 [Y]，變化幅度為 [Z]。

Example 完整示範

The total deforested area increased from 5,000 km² in 1990 to 8,500 km² in 2000, with a change of 3,500 km².

總砍伐面積從1990年的5,000平方公里上升至2000年的8,500平方公里，變化幅度為3,500平方公里。

Pattern B — Steady / Rapid / Slight Change 穩定 / 快速 / 輕微變化

The [variable] rose / fell steadily / rapidly / slightly from [X] to [Y] between [year A] and [year B].

[變量] 在 [年份A] 至 [年份B] 之間穩定 / 快速 / 輕微地從 [X] 上升 / 下降至 [Y]。

Pattern C — Overall Direction + Fluctuation 整體方向 + 波動

Overall, the [variable] showed an upward / downward trend between [year A] and [year B]. However, it fluctuated in between, reaching a peak of [X] in [year] and a trough of [Y] in [year].

整體而言，[變量] 在 [年份A] 至 [年份B] 之間呈現上升 / 下降趨勢。然而期間有所波動，於 [年份] 達到最高點 [X]，於 [年份] 跌至最低點 [Y]。

Key vocabulary 關鍵詞彙： reached a peak of 達到最高點 · fell to a trough of 跌至最低點 · remained stable at 維持穩定在 · levelled off at 趨於平穩在

2 Percentage Change 百分比變化 ▼

Step-by-Step Format 三步格式

Step 1 — Write the formula 寫出公式：

Percentage change = ((New Value – Old Value) / Old Value) × 100%

百分比變化 = ((新數值 – 舊數值) / 舊數值) × 100%

Step 2 — Substitute values 代入數值：

= ((8,500 – 5,000) / 5,000) × 100%

Step 3 — Calculate and state unit 計算並標明單位：

= (3,500 / 5,000) × 100% = 70%

Common Mistakes 常見錯誤

MistakeWrongCorrect
Dividing by New Value(New – Old) / New(New – Old) / Old
Forgetting × 100%3,500 / 5,000 = 0.70.7 × 100% = 70%
Missing the % sign= 70= 70%
Wrong order (Old – New)(5,000 – 8,500) / 5,000(8,500 – 5,000) / 5,000
Marking Scheme Tip: Even if the final answer is wrong, showing the correct formula and substitution can earn method marks. 即使最終答案錯誤，展示正確公式和代入過程仍可獲得方法分。
3 Compare Two Locations 對比兩個地點 ▼

Pattern A — Simple Comparison 簡單對比

Location A has a higher / lower / larger / smaller [feature] than Location B.

地點A的 [特徵] 高於 / 低於 / 大於 / 小於 地點B。

Example

Sai Kung has a higher elevation than Northeast NT, because Sai Kung is composed of volcanic / igneous rocks which are more resistant to weathering.

西貢的海拔高於新界東北，因為西貢由火山/火成岩組成，較抗風化。

Pattern B — Comparison with Reason 對比加原因

[Location A] has a [higher/lower] [feature] than [Location B]. This is because [reason related to rock type / climate / human activity]. As a result, [consequence].

[地點A] 的 [特徵] 較 [地點B] [高/低]。這是因為 [與岩石類型/氣候/人類活動相關的原因]。結果是 [後果]。

Full Example 完整示範

The rocks at High Island have finer crystals than the rocks at Ma On Shan. This is because High Island rocks are extrusive igneous rocks that cooled rapidly above the ground surface, while Ma On Shan rocks are intrusive igneous rocks that cooled slowly underground. As a result, High Island rocks contain small crystals, whereas Ma On Shan rocks contain large crystals.

萬宜水庫的岩石晶體比馬鞍山的岩石更細小。這是因為萬宜水庫的岩石是噴出火成岩，於地表快速冷卻，而馬鞍山的岩石是侵入火成岩，於地下緩慢冷卻。結果是萬宜水庫岩石含細小晶體，馬鞍山岩石含粗大晶體。

4 Highest / Lowest Value 最高 / 最低值 ▼

Basic Pattern 基本句式

The highest / maximum value was [X] in [year], while the lowest / minimum value was [Y] in [year].

最高 / 最大值為 [X]，出現於 [年份]；最低 / 最小值為 [Y]，出現於 [年份]。

Example

The highest annual rainfall was 3,200 mm in 1998, while the lowest was 1,800 mm in 2005.

最高年降雨量為1998年的3,200毫米，最低為2005年的1,800毫米。

Extension — Range 幅度

The range was [X – Y = Z], showing a wide / narrow variation.

幅度為 [X – Y = Z]，顯示變化很大 / 很小。

Example

The range of annual rainfall was 3,200 – 1,800 = 1,400 mm, showing a wide variation.

年降雨量幅度為3,200 – 1,800 = 1,400毫米，顯示變化很大。

5 Cause-Effect Chain (PEEL) 因果關係鏈 ▼

PEEL for Data Response 數據題 PEEL 法

StepMeaning中文
PointState the observation from the data陳述從數據中觀察到的現象
ExplanationExplain the geographical process解釋地理過程
EvidenceQuote specific numbers from the data引用數據中的具體數字
LinkConnect back to the question回扣題目
Full Example — Deforestation in Brazil (1990–2010)

Point: The deforested area in Brazil showed an overall upward trend between 1990 and 2010.
Explanation: This is mainly due to increasing demand for agricultural land and commercial logging activities driven by population growth and rising living standards.
Evidence: According to the data, the deforested area increased from 5,000 km² in 1990 to 8,500 km² in 2000, and further rose to 10,200 km² in 2010.
Link: Therefore, the data clearly shows that deforestation in Brazil worsened significantly during this period, mainly caused by human activities related to economic development.

觀點：巴西的砍伐面積在1990年至2010年之間呈現整體上升趨勢。
解釋：這主要是由於農業用地需求增加以及商業伐木活動，由人口增長和生活水平提高所推動。
證據：根據數據，砍伐面積從1990年的5,000平方公里上升至2000年的8,500平方公里，並進一步上升至2010年的10,200平方公里。
扣題：因此，數據清楚顯示巴西的砍伐情況在此期間顯著惡化，主要由與經濟發展相關的人類活動造成。
