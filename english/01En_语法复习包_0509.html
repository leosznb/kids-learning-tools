🔹 too + adjective（太……了）

公式：too + 形容词 → 表示「太……以至于不合适」
⚠️ too 必须放在形容词前面，不是后面！
🧂
too salty
太咸了
📏
too long
太长了
👕
too short
太短了
😴
too boring
太无聊了
多多易错 The film was boring too. → The film was too boring.（too 在形容词前面！）

🔹 Indefinite Pronouns（不定代词）

指人：someone / anyone / no one / everyone
指物：something / anything / nothing / everything
some-某人/某物
someone
something
any-任何人/物
anyone
anything
no-没人/没东西
no one
nothing
every-每人/每物
everyone
everything
✅ 肯定句用！I saw someone.
⚠️ 疑问句/否定句用！Did you see anyone?
❌ 表示「没有」！No one came.
👥 表示「全部」！Everyone is happy.
⚠️ 不定代词作主语 → 动词用单数！
✅ Everyone is happy.  |  ❌ Everyone are happy.
🤔 快速判断用哪个：
❓ 这句话是疑问句或否定句吗？
✅ 是 → 用 anyone / anything
❌ 不是（是肯定句）→ 用 someone / something 或 everyone / everything 或 no one / nothing

🔹 Possessive Pronouns（物主代词）

Whose + 名词…？→ 回答用名词性物主代词（后面不接名词）
我的
my → mine
你的
your → yours
他的
his → his
她的
her → hers
我们的
our → ours
他们的
their → theirs
口诀：有 s 就是「我的东西」，没 s 要加名词（除了 his 本身就有 s）
多多易错 It is mine book. → It is mine.（mine 后面不接名词！）

🔹 -ing form（动词-ing 作宾语）

like / love / enjoy / hate + V-ing　｜　good at / weak in + V-ing
like reading
love singing
enjoy dancing
hate hiking
good at swimming
weak in riding
多多真实错题 good at do sums → good at doing sums
loved make → loved making
hates listen → hates listening
weak in sing → weak in singing

🔹 Infinitives with 'to'（带 to 的不定式）

would like / want / need + to + 动词原形
多多真实错题 want give up → want to give up（want 后面必须有 to！）

⭐ 最重要单元 · 占分 ~20%

🔹 Present Perfect Tense（现在完成时）

公式：have / has + 过去分词
🎬 动画时间轴：for vs since
📏 for + 一段时间
2008
现在
← for twelve years →
像一段路程：从起点走到现在
📍 since + 时间点
📍2010
现在
since 2010
像一个路标：从那个点开始
🤔 选 for 还是 since？一步一步判断：
① 看后面的词：是「多久」还是「什么时候」？
📏 「多久/多长时间」→ for（for 12 years / for a long time）
📍 「什么时候/哪个点」→ since（since 2010 / since January / since I was born）
多多易错 for January → since January（一月是时间点！） |  since five years → for five years（五年是时间段！）

🔹 Simple Past vs Present Perfect

有关键时间词 → 决定时态
① 句子里有没有「yesterday / ago / last week / in 2016」？
✅ 有 → 一般过去式（Simple Past）
例：I won an award in 2016. / I saw the movie yesterday.
② 句子里有没有「for / since / ever / never / yet / just / already」？
✅ 有 → 现在完成式（Present Perfect）
例：He has won three awards since 2016. / I have never been to the USA.
🔑 关键信号词速查表
→ 一般过去式→ 现在完成式
yesterday, last night/week/monthfor (一段时间), since (时间点)
... ago (two days ago)ever, never
in 2016, in 2020 (过去年份)yet (否定/问句), just, already
just now, this morning (已过去)so far, up to now, before
多多最常错 Clara wrote 20 songs since 2020. → Clara has written 20 songs since 2020.
I have seen that movie yesterday. → I saw that movie yesterday.

🔹 Infinitives with / without 'to'

need / want / would like / learn + to + V　｜　let / make / see / watch / hear + 人 + V（不带to）
✅ 带 to

want to get

learn to be
try to help
🚫 不带 to（bare infinitive）
let sb have
make sb feel
see sb walk
watch sb do
hear sb talk
多多易错 Ivy let the old lady to have her seat. → Ivy let the old lady have her seat.（let 后面不带 to！）
