📋 U1 Quick Rules · 不定代名詞 + too + adj
some- (someone/something/somewhere) → 肯定句 | any- → 否定句/疑問句 | no- → 否定意思（動詞用肯定）
every- → 「每一個」| too + adj → 「太……」（負面：太…而不能）

1 somebody / anybody / nobody / everybody · 10題
1
I knocked on the door but _____ answered.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？「but」表示轉折——敲了門，但沒人應門。nobody = 沒有人。
2
Is there _____ in the classroom?
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？疑問句用 any- → anybody。somebody 只用於肯定句。
3
_____ loves the new teacher. She is very kind.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？「She is very kind」說明老師很好，所以「每個人都喜歡她」。everybody = 每個人。
4
_____ called you while you were out. It was your mum.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？肯定句 + 有具體的人 → somebody（有人）。後面確認是媽媽。
5
I don't think _____ can finish this in one minute.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？「don't think」是否定語氣 → 用 any- → anybody。anybody = 任何人（否定句中 = 沒有人能）。
6
_____ wants to play with Tom because he is always mean.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？Tom 總是 mean（刻薄），所以「沒有人」想跟他玩。nobody + 肯定動詞 = 沒有人。
7
Is _____ ready? Let's start the game!
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？Let's start = 要開始遊戲了 → 問「大家都準備好了嗎」。everyone = 每個人。
8
There is _____ at the door. Can you open it?
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？肯定句 → someone（有人）。後文「Can you open it?」確認門外有人。
9
Did you see _____ strange in the park yesterday?
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？疑問句 → any-。strange 是「奇怪的」，指事物 → anything。「something」只用於肯定句。
10
The room was empty. _____ was there.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？empty = 空的 → 沒有人。nobody + was = 沒有人。如果要用 anybody 必須說 "There wasn't anybody"。

19

20
Don't worry. _____ will be fine.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？Don't worry = 別擔心 → 一切都會好的。everything = 一切（所有事情）。

📋 U2 Quick Rules · 所有格代名詞 + 介詞
Possessive Pronouns（獨立使用，不加名詞）：mine, yours, his, hers, ours, theirs

1 mine / yours / his / hers / ours / theirs · 10題
1
This book is not _____. It belongs to Tom.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？is not ___ → 需要名詞性物主代名詞（獨立使用）。my 必須加名詞（my book），mine 可以獨立使用。
2
This red pen is _____. I bought it yesterday.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？is ___ → 獨立使用，不加名詞 → mine。
3
Is this umbrella _____? You left it here last week.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？Is this umbrella ___?（對 you 說話）→ yours。your 必須加名詞（your umbrella）。
4
The blue car over there is _____. My parents bought it last month.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？My parents bought = 我們的家庭 → ours。our 必須加名詞（our car）。
5
These gloves belong to my sister. They are _____.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？my sister（女性、第三人稱）→ hers。her 必須加名詞（her gloves）。
6
John and Peter said the football was _____.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？John and Peter = 他們（兩人以上）→ theirs。his 只指一個人（男）。
7
My lunchbox is blue. _____ is red.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？My lunchbox... ___ is red → 對比「我的」和「你的」。Yours 作主語，獨立使用。
8
That dog is not _____. Ours has a longer tail.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？Ours has... = 我們的狗 → 對比 → 那隻狗不是「他們的」。theirs。
9
I found a wallet on the floor. Is it _____?
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？Is it ___?（問對方）→ yours。your 必須加名詞。
10
This isn't my book. _____ has a red cover.
✅ Correct!❌ Wrong!
為什麼？___ has a red cover → 作主語 → Mine（我的書）。My 不能單獨作主語。

📋 U3 Quick Rules · 動名詞 vs 不定式
動名詞 (-ing)：enjoy, finish, keep, mind, practice, suggest + -ing
不定式 (to + V)：want, hope, decide, plan, agree, promise + to V
兩者皆可：like, love, hate, start, begin + -ing / to V（意思相近）
動名詞作主語：Swimming is fun.（游泳很有趣）

📋 U5 Quick Rules · so many/much + -ed/-ing adjectives
so many + 可數名詞複數（so many books, so many people）
so much + 不可數名詞（so much water, so much time）
-ed 形容詞 → 人的感受（I am interested. / She is bored.）
-ing 形容詞 → 事物的特性（The book is interesting. / The film is boring.）

📋 U6 Quick Rules · 不帶to的不定式 + ought to
let/make/help + 賓語 + 不帶to的不定式（原形動詞）
　→ Let me go. / Make him stop. / Help her clean.
情態動詞 (can, could, will, would, shall, should, may, might, must) + 原形動詞
ought to + 原形動詞（= should，但語氣更強）
