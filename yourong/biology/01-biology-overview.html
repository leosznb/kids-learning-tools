🔴 Topic 1: Food & Nutrition 食物与营养
7 food substances — carbohydrates, lipids, proteins, vitamins, minerals, dietary fibre, water
Food tests — Benedict’s (reducing sugar), Iodine (starch), Biuret (protein), Spot test (lipids), DCPIP (vitamin C)
Deficiency diseases — scurvy (vit C), night blindness (vit A), rickets (vit D/calcium), anaemia (iron), goitre (iodine), kwashiorkor (protein)
Energy values — carbs/proteins ~17kJ/g, lipids ~38kJ/g, water 0kJ
BMI — Weight(kg) ÷ Height²(m²)
Food pyramid — Grains → Vegetables/Fruits → Meat/Dairy → Fats/Sugar/Salt

🔴 Topic 2: Digestion & Absorption 消化与吸收
Alimentary canal — Mouth → Oesophagus → Stomach → Small intestine → Large intestine → Anus
Digestive juices table — Saliva (salivary amylase), Gastric juice (HCl, pepsin, mucus), Pancreatic juice (lipase, amylase, protease), Bile (bile salts → emulsification 乳化, NOT an enzyme!), Intestinal juice
Absorption @ villi — Blood vessel A: glucose/amino acids/minerals/vit B&C → liver → heart; Lymph vessel C: fatty acids/glycerol/fat-soluble vitamins
Physical vs Chemical digestion — Bile emulsification = physical; Enzyme breakdown = chemical
🟡 Topic 3: Enzymes 酶
Key facts — Biological catalysts, proteins, reusable (NOT used up!), specific (lock-and-key model, active site)
Factors — Temperature, pH; denaturation (高温/极端pH, 不可逆) ≠ inactivation (低温, 可逆)
Common mistakes — ❌ "enzymes killed by high temp" → ✅ denatured; ❌ "enzymes used up" → ✅ reusable; ❌ "all enzymes optimum 37°C" → ✅ different enzymes = different optimum
Application — Biological washing powder (lipase + protease)

🔴 Topic 4: Body Defence & Diseases 身体防御与疾病
Disease types — Infectious (bacteria/virus/fungi/protozoa) vs Non-infectious
Transmission — Vector-borne (malaria, dengue), Airborne (flu, COVID-19), Waterborne (cholera), Contact
Immunity curves — Primary response = slow + low; Secondary response = fast + high (memory cells!)
Active (infection/vaccination → own antibodies) vs Passive (injected antibodies)
Cancer risk matching — Colorectal↔diet high fat/low fibre, Lung↔smoking/asbestos, Skin↔UV, Liver↔alcohol
Cardiovascular disease — Lipid deposit → atherosclerosis → coronary artery blockage
Diabetes — Blood glucose graph: normal recovers in 2h; diabetic stays high; Insulin injection NOT oral
🟡 Topic 5: DNA & Biotechnology DNA与生物科技
DNA structure — Nucleotide = phosphate + deoxyribose + base (A-T, C-G), double helix, hydrogen bonds
DNA charge — Negatively charged → moves to positive terminal in gel electrophoresis → smaller fragments travel further/faster
DNA fingerprinting — extract DNA → obtain fragments → gel electrophoresis → band pattern matching
DNA extraction steps — Detergent (break membrane) → Salt (neutralize charge) → Ice bath (prevent DNase) → Ethanol (precipitate DNA)
GM insulin advantages — Cheaper, no rejection, high yield (NOT low yield!)
