D Reading Comprehension (12 marks, @2 marks)

Read the following article and answer ALL questions.

A Hero in Our City Jason Chan is a 35-year-old firefighter. He has worked at Mong Kok Fire Station since 2012. Jason has always wanted to help people, and he loves being a firefighter. Last December, there was a big fire in an old building. Jason and his team rushed to the scene. They saw an elderly woman standing at the window on the fifth floor. She could not get out by herself. "I saw the woman shouting for help. I knew I had to act fast," Jason said. He climbed up the ladder and carried the woman to safety. The woman was very frightened, but Jason told her to stay calm. Jason has saved many people since he became a firefighter. He has won three bravery awards. He says, "Being a firefighter is not just a job. It is about helping people when they need it most." Jason thinks everyone ought to learn basic fire safety. "You ought to check your smoke alarms regularly. You ought not to leave cooking unattended," he reminds the public. Jason is truly a person we can all admire.
