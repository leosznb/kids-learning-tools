Writing Task An Unforgettable Experience

Choose ONE of the following topics. Write at least 80 words.

Topic A: An Unforgettable School Event 🏫

You have taken part in a school event recently (e.g. Sports Day, Open Day, a school trip, a performance). Write a diary entry about it.

You should include:

  • What was the event? When and where did it happen?
  • What did you do / see / hear?
  • How did you feel? Why was it unforgettable?

Topic B: An Unforgettable Family Memory 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Write about a special memory with your family (e.g. a holiday, a celebration, a day out). Write it as a short story.

You should include:

  • What happened? When and where did it take place?
  • Who was with you? What did you do together?
  • How did everyone feel? Why do you still remember it?

• I have never felt so … before.
• It was the most unforgettable experience I have ever had.

🌟 Model Answer — Topic A (School Sports Day)

Sunday, 8th June Dear Diary, Today was the school Sports Day, and it was the most exciting day I have ever had! When I arrived at school, I saw so many students wearing their house T-shirts. There was so much noise and laughter everywhere. I felt a little worried because I was in the 100-metre race. When the race started, I ran as fast as I could. I heard my friends shouting my name. In the end, I came second! I was so excited that I almost cried. My classmates were very touched when I showed them my medal. The whole day was really tiring, but it was also the best day of my life. I will never forget this Sports Day because I learned that trying your best is more important than winning. This experience has taught me to believe in myself. (Words: ~120)
語法亮點得分原因
exciting (事物)正確使用 -ing 形容學校活動
so many studentsmany + 可數複數
so much noisemuch + 不可數
worried, excited, touched-ed 形容個人感受（3個不同感受詞）
tiring-ing 形容事物的性質
have ever had現在完成時（U4）

🌟 Model Answer — Topic B (Family Memory)

Last summer, my family went to Cheung Chau for a day trip. It was an unforgettable experience that I still think about today. We took the ferry early in the morning. There were so many people on the ferry! When we arrived, I was very excited because I had never been there before. We walked along the beach and ate so much delicious food, like fishballs and mango mochi. In the afternoon, we rented bicycles and rode around the island. The view was amazing. My little sister was tired after cycling, so Dad carried her on his back. Seeing that made me feel very touched. The whole trip was tiring but wonderful. I will always remember this day because I spent it with the people I love most. I have been to many places since then, but this trip has remained my favourite family memory. (Words: ~135)
語法亮點得分原因
so many peoplemany + 可數複數
so much delicious foodmuch + 不可數
excited, tired, touched-ed 形容感受（3個不同詞）
amazing, tiring-ing 形容事物
have been, has remained現在完成時連接過去與現在（U4）
完整句子結構每句都有主語+動詞，符合香港評卷要求

📌 家長指導筆記

批改時的重點檢查：

  1. -ed / -ing 有沒有搞反？ —— 多多最常錯（I am interesting → interested）
  2. so many / so much 有沒有用對？ —— 後面名詞可數還是不可數？
  3. 有沒有寫完整句子？ —— 不能只寫關鍵詞，每句要有主語+動詞
  4. 過去時態是否一致？ —— 講過去的事，動詞都要用過去式
  5. 有沒有至少 2-3 個不同的感受詞？ —— 從詞彙提示中選
